Experiences

  • Laboratoire Informatique Avignon (LIA) | Avignon, France | 2nd Year Undergraduate Intern | 2 mo. 2021

    I worked under the supervision of Assoc. Prof Titouan Parcollet (LIA and Cambridge visitor).

    I have investigated differents ways of making LiGRU a mandatory alternative to LSTM/GRU for speech processing. There is theoretical and empirical evidence that LiGRU simply is better than LSTM and GRU. Unfortunately, LiGRU suffers from poor GPU implementation and instability in the recurrent connection.

    I reduced by 4x the computation time of the LiGRU by implementing from scratch the Backpropagation Through Time equations in PyTorch with torch.autograd.Function and by combining torch.jit.script and Cuda custom kernels with CuPy.

    The work has led to a pull request (currently being reviewed) into SpeechBrain which is an open-source and all-in-one conversational AI toolkit with more than 4k stars on Github.

    Stack used : Python - PyTorch, CuPy, Numba, Triton (openAI), OVH, Docker.

  • E-Magina | Avignon, France | 1st Year Undergraduate Intern | 1 mo. 2020

    I have been asked to conduct an extensive data scrapping, analysis and modelling of an online french sports club directory. One other aspect of the work was to carefully design and experiment a System Multi-Agent to automatically classify a club description with the right associated sports.
